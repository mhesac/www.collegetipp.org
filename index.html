Tuition Installment Payment Plan

A College Education...One Payment at a Time.

Tuition installment payment plans are making college more affordable for students throughout Montana.

How do tuition installment payment plans help students and families?

  • Instead of making two or three lump sum payments, plan participants can make timely, smaller payments for the academic year over a period of 10 months.
  • The opportunity to make smaller payments on a monthly basis allows many students and families to minimize the amount they need to borrow in student loans, including PLUS loans.
  • Payments are interest free.
