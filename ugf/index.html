Tuition Installment Payment Plan

What is a tuition installment payment plan?

A Tuition Installment Payment Plan - as offered to you by your school and Higher Education Services, Inc. - allows students and families to make monthly payments to a school for tuition, and room and board. Usually these installment payments are spread over 5 months per semester, beginning August 1 for Fall semester and January 1 for Spring semester.

How does it help students and families?

  • Instead of making a tuition payment for the full amount, plan participants can make smaller payments over a period of 5 months per semester.
  • The opportunity to make smaller payments on a monthly basis makes the cost of higher education more manageable and minimizes the amount that families need to borrow in student loans.
  • Participation does not affect eligibility for financial aid.
  • Payments are interest free; a small, one-time enrollment fee is required.
